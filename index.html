Spinach Scrambled Eggs

If breakfast is the most important meal of the day? WHy not make it nutritionally dense and something you can enjoy. This recipe is not only good for you it takes 10 minutes to make. Just follow along with these instructions and watch it all come together

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • spinach
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Whisk together 3 eggs,cheese, and spinach
  2. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Add butter to pan on medium-high heat
  4. Once hot, add egg mixture cook for 5-10 minutes
  5. Plate and enjoy!

    Garlic Lemon Chicken Breast

    This recipe will require some planning ahead of time. You will have to marinate the chicken overnight to get the best results, but it will be so worth it if you do. If marinating sounds intimidating to you just follow these easy steps and you'll be done before you even know we started.

    Ingredients

    • Chicken breast
    • lemon
    • olive oil
    • oregano
    • basil
    • salt
    • pepper
    • garlic

    Steps

    1. Mince Garlic
    2. Add chicken to large bowl
    3. Add garlic,chicken, and seasonings to bowl
    4. Place lid on bowl and set in the fridge
    5. When ready to cook, place chicken in pan on medium to medium-high heat
    6. Cook 3-5 minutes per side,until chicken is browned
